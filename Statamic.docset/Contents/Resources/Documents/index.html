Welcome to Statamic

This docset is your complete guide to the world of Statamic.

Statamic is a flat-file content management system that makes mixing static and dynamic elements simple (and, dare we say, fun). It’s built on PHP, but you don’t need to know or write any PHP to use it. It takes very little to run, and we think you’re going to love it.

Statamic is not a static-site generator. It’s live, quick, and dynamic.
